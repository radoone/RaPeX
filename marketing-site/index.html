Critical

Trust loss is harder to recover

How it works

Simple workflow, merchant-facing output.

1

Sync the alert source

The backend imports fresh records from the EU Safety Gate data feed and keeps them searchable for matching.

2

Check products automatically or on demand

Product creates, updates, manual checks, and bulk runs all feed normalized Shopify product data into the checker.

3

Review and act

Merchants get an alert with similarity context so they can inspect the match, contact the supplier, or remove the listing.
